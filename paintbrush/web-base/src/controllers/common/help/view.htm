Frequently Asked Questions

How does ActivCanvas Work?

ActivCanvas is an image recognition app that allows audiences to access hidden audiovisual content by scanning an activated artwork with their smartphone. Artists can select an artwork(s) and activate it with relevant documentary footage connected with that specific piece. Through the platform they can activate that artwork so that audiences can access the footage through the ActivCanvas app wherever that artwork may be in the real world, in a gallery, on a screen, or in print. You can change, update, and deactivate, the content behind your work through your Art Retail Network Profile.

How do I deactivate my account?

To permanently delete your account please contact the team through support@artretailnetwork.com. Please include within your email. Your full name,email address with which your account is connected, and the type of your subscription package if you currently hold one. The team will answer your enquiry promptly and let you know of the successful deletion.

How do I stop the payments for my ActivCanvas Subscription?

To stop your subscription please contact the team through support@artretailnetwork.com. Please include within your email. Your full name,email address with which your account is connected, and the type of subscription package you currently hold. The team will answer your enquiry promptly and let you know of the successful subscription deactivation. We are working towards automating deactivation to make it self-service in future.

What Smartphones does ActivCanvas work with?

ActivCanvas currently works on Android 4.03 upwards and iOS 7 and upwards. If you have an issue downloading the app please contact support@artretailnetwork.com. Our team are constantly improving accessibility and functionality of the app across platforms.

How do I edit one of my artworks?

To edit an artwork you need to be open on your profile page of the platform. Once on your profile hold your cursor over the artwork you wish to edit and you will see a red button, with a pencil icon, appear in its top corner. When you click on this red button it will open a pop up where you can edit all the information fields for that specific artwork. Remember to save changes.

What are the image requirements for uploading an artwork successfully?

Images need to be either a JPG or PNG file no larger than 10MB. Try and take a well lit, high resolution image that is cropped so that any framing, background, or supporting wall is cut out of the image and only the artwork itself is included.

What are Tags?

When you upload an artwork, aside from completing fields such as ‘Title’ and ‘Medium’ , you can tag your artwork with key terms related to it (eg. ‘Trees’, ‘Scotland’, ‘Shadows’). People are able to search the Marketplace using key terms. The tags that you use to describe your artwork will help these searches find appropriate artwork.

How do I delete artwork from my profile?

To delete a piece of art go to your profile and hover your cursor over the artwork you wish to delete. You will see a red button, with a pencil icon, appear in its top corner. When you click on this red button it will open a pop up where you can edit all the information fields for that specific artwork. Within this pop up you need to click on the Delete button

Can I upload videos from Youtube?

Unfortunately at the moment you cannot upload content straight from Youtube. You will need to download the video first then upload it onto the site. We are hoping to build a Youtube functionality into the platform soon.

Artwork

Adding Artwork

To add artwork go to your profile page, navigate to the artwork tab then click on the upload button.

Please be as descriptive and accurate as possible when filling out any fields

Image Requirements

  • A .jpg or .png file
  • No larger than 10MB
  • Ideally cropped so any frame/background wall is removed

Tags

Tags allow you to categorise artwork in a more flexible manner than a fixed list of subjects/styles allow.

Your artwork will be made searchable using these tags so choosing descriptive tags will help other users discover your work.

Styles/Subjects/Materials

These are multiselect fields, please choose any items that apply up to the maximum allowed per field.

Updating Artwork

To update existing artwork go to your profile page, navigate to the artwork tab.

Find the piece you want to update then click on the edit button ().

Removing Artwork

To delete a piece of artwork follow the steps for updating, once the edit form has loaded click the delete button at the bottom of the form.
