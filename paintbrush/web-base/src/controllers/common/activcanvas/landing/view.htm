ActivCanvas makes it possible for anyone to discover the authentic story of any enabled artwork simply by pointing their smartphone at it. The app recognizes the image and triggers a video telling the story of the artwork. ActivCanvas helps artists, galleries and art fairs sell more art by connecting the buyers and collectors through the artist's story.
